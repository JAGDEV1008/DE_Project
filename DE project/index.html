Adders in Digital Electronics

Adders are digital circuits used to perform addition of binary numbers. Half Adder adds two single-bit binary numbers and gives a Sum and Carry output. Full Adder adds three binary inputs (including carry-in) and produces Sum and Carry-out. These are essential components in the design of arithmetic and logic units in processors.
